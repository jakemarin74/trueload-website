HOW IT WORKS SECTION
====================

Section label: How It Works

Section headline: Six steps from job site to approved report

Section body: TrueLoad walks you through the entire NEC 220.87 process — from requesting utility data to printing a report ready for the AHJ.

Step 1 title: Request AMI data from the utility
Step 1 body: TrueLoad provides a pre-written email template your customer sends to the utility. The utility sends the interval data file directly to you.

Step 2 title: Create a new project
Step 2 body: One project per job site. Enter the customer information, your preparer details, and the existing service specifications.

Step 3 title: Enter the existing service
Step 3 body: Main breaker size and service voltage. TrueLoad uses these to determine total service capacity in kVA.

Step 4 title: Add proposed equipment
Step 4 body: List each piece of new equipment — heat pump, EV charger, water heater — and mark whether it's concurrent with the measured peak.

Step 5 title: Upload the AMI data file
Step 5 body: Drag and drop the file from the utility — CSV, Excel, or XML. TrueLoad processes it automatically and identifies the peak demand.

Step 6 title: Review results and print the report
Step 6 body: PASS or FAIL is displayed immediately. Print a formatted report ready for permit submission directly from the browser.
